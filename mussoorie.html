Mussoorie Heights

Day 1 - Arrival in Mussoorie

Hotel check-in. After relaxing, visit to Camel's Back Road, located close to city center. Explore 180 year
old british cemetery. Enjoy scenic view, sunset. Head back to your hotel.

Day 2 - Visit to Kempty Falls & Company Garden

Breakfast at hotel. After breakfast, we will take you to Kempty Falls, one of the most prominent tourist
attractions. Explore the hilly topography of Kempty by ridding on the Kempty Falls Ropeway. After that,
we will take you to Company Garden, a fun park, ideal place to visit with family. There is a play area for
children. Explore the Company Garden nursery, and enjoy the boat ride along tha artificial lake.

Day 3 - Explore Dhanaulti

Wake up early on day 3 as you will be heading to scenic heaven of Dhanaulti. Visit to Surkhanda Devi Temple
and get a glimps of Mussoorie culture. Head over to Eco Park and enjoy the nature. Now head back to hotel

Day 4 - Shopping at Mussoorie Mall Road, Gandola Ride followed by Departure

Walk through Mussoorie Mall Road and indulge in shopping. Children can enjoy video-game parlours & skating rinks.
Enjoy the Gandola Ride. Dress up in traditional Mussoorie costume and click photographs. Now its time to bid
goodbye to Mussoorie and take back home memories to cherish!
